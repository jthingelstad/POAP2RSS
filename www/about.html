About this project

POAP2RSS was created to make POAP events easier to follow and integrate with the wider web. By turning event activity into RSS, you can plug POAP claims into any reader or automation workflow.

This service is open-source and maintained by Jamie Thingelstad (aka poap.thingelstad.eth).

Jamie has many POAP events and always wanted a way to know when tokens were claimed. He also has a number of friends that are active with POAP and wanted a way to know when they collected new events. POAP2RSS fills that gap!
