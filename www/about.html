About this project

POAP2RSS was created to make POAP events easier to follow and integrate with the wider web. By turning event activity into RSS, you can plug POAP claims into any reader or automation workflow.

This service is open-source and maintained by Jamie Thingelstad (poap.thingelstad.eth).
